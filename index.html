Meet the Artist



Hi there, I'm Thea. I am an artist, illustration and printmaking, and a college student who is tryingzz to survive in the world. My work ranges from real world to imaginary. Creatures, people, and random doodles that bloosomed into a full idea.

My piece of advice is a sketchbook should be treated like a scrapbook, journal, and a space for you to express yourself and your art. Make it a place where you feel safe and comfortable to explore your art.
